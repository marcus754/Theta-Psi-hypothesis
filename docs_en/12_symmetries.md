12. SYMMETRIES AND PROHIBITIONS OF Theta–Psi

This document fixes not just "which symmetries are beautiful," but which ones must be present in a working version of the hypothesis if time is understood as a positive physical object rather than a pure scale.

1. EXACT SYMMETRIES

1.1. Diffeomorphism Invariance
The full theory is assumed to be coordinate-independent:
x^mu -> x'^mu(x), S[Theta, Psi, g] invariant.
This is a mandatory base symmetry of the gravitational sector.

1.2. Internal Phase Symmetry of Psi
If Psi remains a complex energy field, the minimal internal symmetry is:
Psi -> e^iα Psi.
It naturally leads to a conserved charge and justifies the use of amplitude and phase in reduced sectors.

2. SPONTANEOUSLY BROKEN SYMMETRIES

2.1. Lorentz Symmetry of the Background
The action may be Lorentz-covariant, but a specific Theta_munu background selects a preferred temporal direction u^mu. This means:
Lorentz is exact at the action level,
Lorentz is spontaneously broken by the background solution.
Specifically, such a portrait is most natural for the "time as a medium" hypothesis.

2.2. FRW Symmetry as a Symmetry of the Solution
Homogeneity and isotropy should not be exact symmetries of the full theory. These are symmetries of the cosmological solution:
FRW: homogeneous/isotropic branch of Theta–Psi.
Strong field: generic inhomogeneous branch.

3. FORBIDDEN OR NON-PHYSICAL "SYMMETRIES"

3.1. No Physical Z2-Symmetry in Time
If time is positive by its nature, then the transformation
theta_eff -> -theta_eff
is not a physical symmetry of the canon. This is a key prohibition.

3.2. No Permutation Symmetry Theta <-> Psi
Theta_munu and Psi are non-equivalent in their roles:
- Theta_munu sets the causal-temporal regime;
- Psi carries the energy content.
Therefore, the theory should not allow an exchange symmetry of the form
Theta <-> Psi.

3.3. No Exact "Zero Time" Symmetry
The state theta_eff = 0 is not considered an ordinary point of the configuration space through which the theory can be analytically continued. It is a boundary of the physically allowed region.

4. EFFECTIVE/RESIDUAL SYMMETRIES

4.1. Scaling Residual Symmetry of the Reduced Bridge
If the canon uses
Phi_eff^can = ln(theta_eff / theta_0),
then a residual symmetry of joint scaling is natural:
theta_eff -> c theta_eff,
theta_0 -> c theta_0.
It does not have to be fundamental but serves as a structural hint: physics depends on the ratio of the regime to the reference level, not on the "absolute zero" of time.

5. WHAT THE SYMMETRY PORTRAIT PROHIBITS IN THE LAGRANGIAN

If the current canon is correct, the following are suspicious or prohibited:
- terms insensitive to the positivity of theta_eff;
- terms symmetric under theta_eff -> -theta_eff;
- direct permutation terms making Theta and Psi equivalent;
- strong explicit violations of Diff(M);
- arbitrary non-universal couplings of matter to n, breaking equivalence.

6. OPERATIONAL CONCLUSION

The Theta–Psi symmetry portrait should now be read as:
- Diff(M) — exact;
- U(1)_Psi — a natural exact/almost exact internal symmetry;
- Lorentz symmetry — covariance of the action + spontaneous breaking by the background;
- FRW — symmetry of a particular solution;
- theta_eff -> -theta_eff — not a physical symmetry;
- Theta <-> Psi — forbidden;
- theta_eff = 0 — boundary of the allowed physical region, not an ordinary branch.
